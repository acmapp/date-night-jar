Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

Date Night Jar ("the App") is developed by Andrew Mitchell. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Period.

Local Storage

All content you create — date ideas, schedules, jar preferences, decorations, and drawings — is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics & Tracking

The App contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party services of any kind. It does not connect to the internet.

Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party services, APIs, or frameworks that collect data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
