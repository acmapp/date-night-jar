What's Inside

Shake the Jar

Tap to shake — watch your ideas bounce around with real physics, then draw one at random. Mark it done or draw again.

Your Ideas, Your Way

Start with 12 curated date ideas or add your own. Tag them by mood — Romantic, Active, Cozy, Creative — then search and filter.

Schedule Date Nights

Pick a specific date or set up recurring nights — weekly, biweekly, or monthly. The app celebrates with confetti when it's date night.

Draw & Decorate

Doodle freehand with your finger or Apple Pencil. Place stickers — trees, cacti, hearts, stars. Pinch, rotate, and drag to make it yours.

4 Hand-Illustrated Jars

Classic Mason, Round Bubble, Tall Vintage, or Wide Cookie. Pick your jar, choose light bulbs or paper balls, and adjust the size.

Name Your Jar

Call it whatever you want. Your names, a nickname, an inside joke — with adjustable font size so it looks just right.

Private by Design

No accounts. No internet. No tracking. All your data stays on your device. Always.
