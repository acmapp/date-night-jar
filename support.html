Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
Everything is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud or shared with anyone.

Can I transfer my data to a new phone?
Your data transfers automatically when you restore from an iCloud or local backup during device setup.

How do I delete all my data?
Delete the app. All data is removed with it.

The app is crashing or not working correctly.
Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, delete and reinstall. If you're still having trouble, contact us below.
